📖 How to Use

Getting Started

1. Save the SUTRA WhatsApp number in your contacts
2. Send "START" to begin setup
3. Follow the onboarding instructions
4. Start sending orders via WhatsApp

Example Commands

• "Mera stock batao" - Show my inventory
• "Aaj kitne orders hue?" - How many orders today?
• "Mohan ko 5 thān white cotton bhej do" - Send 5 thān white cotton to Mohan
• "Mujhe yaad karo ki Sharma ji ₹5000 udhaar hai" - Remember Sharma ji's credit
